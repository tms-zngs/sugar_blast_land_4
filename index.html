How to Play

Easy to Learn, Sweet to Master

  • Match 3 or more candies of the same color to crush them.

  • Match 4+ candies to generate boosters like candy bombs or striped treats.

  • Combine boosters for massive chain explosions.

  • Complete level goals to unlock rewards and progress through candy regions.

Start simple, then unlock wild combos as your skills grow!

Candy Combos

Unleash the Sweetest Explosions

Strategic matches will unlock powerful effects:

  • Striped Candy – Clears a full row or column.

  • Wrapped Candy – Explodes in a 3x3 area twice.

  • Color Bomb – Destroys all candies of one color.

  • Combo Mixes – Combine two boosters for super blasts!

The more you match, the juicier the explosions.
