Candy Combos

Unleash the Sweetest Explosions

Strategic matches will unlock powerful effects:

  • Striped Candy – Clears a full row or column.

  • Wrapped Candy – Explodes in a 3x3 area twice.

  • Color Bomb – Destroys all candies of one color.

  • Combo Mixes – Combine two boosters for super blasts!

The more you match, the juicier the explosions.
